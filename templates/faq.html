Help & FAQ

Everything you need to know about using the Y6 Practice Exam System

Getting Started

1

Login to Your Account

Use your email and password provided by your teacher or administrator to login. If you received an email with a magic link, simply click the link to login automatically.

2

View Your Dashboard

Once logged in, you'll see your dashboard with pending exams, completed exams, and your overall performance statistics.

3

Start an Exam

Click on any pending exam to start. Read each question carefully and select or type your answer. Your progress is saved automatically.

4

Submit and Wait for Results

When you're done, click Submit. Multiple choice questions are graded automatically. Written answers will be reviewed by your teacher.

Frequently Asked Questions

What subjects are covered?
The Y6 Practice Exam system covers four subjects: English, Mathematics, ICT (Information and Communication Technology), and Science. All content follows the Cambridge curriculum standards.
How are exams graded?
Multiple choice and fill-in-the-blank questions are graded automatically. Written answers require manual review by your teacher. You'll receive your results once all questions have been graded and your teacher releases the results.
Can I save my progress and continue later?
Yes! Your answers are automatically saved as you type. If you need to leave and come back, your progress will be waiting for you. However, once you submit an exam, you cannot make changes.
How do I view my results?
Once your teacher releases the results, you can view them from your dashboard. Click on any completed exam to see your score, correct answers, and any feedback from your teacher.
What if I forgot my password?
Please contact your teacher or school administrator to reset your password. They can provide you with a new login or send you a magic link to access your account.
Can I see the correct answers after submitting?
Yes, but only after your teacher releases the results. This allows you to learn from your mistakes and understand the correct solutions.
What happens if my internet disconnects during an exam?
Don't worry! Your answers are saved automatically. Simply log back in and continue from where you left off. Your progress is preserved.
How long do I have to complete an exam?
The time limit depends on each exam. You can see the duration when you start the exam. If there's a deadline, make sure to submit before it passes.

For Parents

How can I track my child's progress?
Contact your child's teacher to receive regular progress updates. Teachers can provide reports on exam scores, areas of strength, and areas that need improvement.
Is the system safe for children?
Yes, the Y6 Practice Exam system is designed specifically for Year 6 students. There is no social interaction features, and all content is educational and age-appropriate.
Can I help my child during exams?
While you can help your child understand how to use the system, we recommend letting them complete exams independently. This helps teachers accurately assess their understanding and provide appropriate support.

Need More Help?

If you have questions that aren't answered here, please contact your teacher or school administrator.
